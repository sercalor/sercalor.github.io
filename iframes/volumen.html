Volumen Sintetico

Exhibited in Matucana 100, Santiago de Chile 2008, hand made in Beijing 2007.

Volumen Sintetico is an acoustic weapon made from 1620 earbuds. This sound sculpture is a mechanical amplifier that bends the sound of small earbuds (seemingly powerless and normally hidden listening devices) into a focal point in which the sound becomes unbearably loud and public.
